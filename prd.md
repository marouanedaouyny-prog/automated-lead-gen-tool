# PRD: Automated Lead Gen Tool

## 1. Project Overview
A B2B lead generation tool that scrapes data from LinkedIn, company websites, and public directories to build lists of potential clients based on specific criteria.

## 2. Target Audience
- Sales Teams
- Marketing Agencies
- Business Consultants

## 3. Key Features
- **Lead Discovery:** Search for leads by industry, location, and job title.
- **Email Verification:** Automatically verify found email addresses to ensure high deliverability.
- **Bulk Export:** Export lead lists to CSV, Google Sheets, or CRM systems.
- **Daily Updates:** Scheduled scraping to fetch the latest leads matching your search.

## 4. Tech Stack (CV Reference)
- **Scraping Engine:** Python with Selenium/Playwright (Handling LinkedIn/Dynamic sites).
- **Backend:** Node.js with API integration for email verification services.
- **Frontend Dashboard:** Next.js and Tailwind CSS (Lead management and search).
- **Database:** MongoDB for lead storage and deduplication.

## 5. Roadmap
- **MVP:** LinkedIn scraping for a single keyword and CSV export.
- **V1:** Email discovery/verification and multi-source scraping.
- **V2:** Direct CRM integration (Salesforce/HubSpot) and automated cold emailing.
